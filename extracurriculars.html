Extra-Curriculars

Hindu Students Council (HSC)
Secretary (Current)

Being part of the Boston University HSC has taught me how to work in a team to design and organize large-scaled events. I've learnt to be more creative to bring something new to our events such as Holi and Garba. Above all, the most rewarding thing is to see the smile on peoples faces, even if they are from another culture, when they come to celebrate festivals and emerse themselves in the Hindu culture. It is truly worth working hard for.



Entrepreneurship Club (E-Club)
Member and Mentor (Past)

In my second semester at Boston University, I joined the E-Club challenge where we had to create a business proposal for a new company in a team of 4-5 students. The challenge was one of the most exciting competitions I had ever been to because we had worked really hard to show off our idea. As a team of mostly engineers, our idea was an phone app that was tailored to clubs. It would include both a news feed, and an integrated calendar to keep track of meetings and talks. Our app, which we called Guild, won second place and the attention of many. However, due to timing constraints in our team schedule, we could not develop the app.



Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV)
Member (Past)

The UAV team was one that will always hold a special place in my heart. However, due to my busy schedule, I could not continue to be a part of it. The drone we built helped me gain a lot of knowledge in electrical engineering, and introduced me to computer aided design (CAD) and programming in Arduino.
